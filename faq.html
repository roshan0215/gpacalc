GPA Calculator FAQ

How to Use the GPA Calculator:

1. Enter the number of years you want to log data for.

2. Click the "Create Table" button to generate the data entry table.

3. Enter the number of classes you are taking for each semester.

4. For each class, enter the course name, grade, and check the "Honors/AP" box if applicable.

5. The GPA will be automatically calculated and displayed below the table.

6. You can save, load, and clear data using the respective buttons.

7. Click the "FAQ" button to view these instructions again.

How is the data saved?

The data is saved in your browser, and the data inputted is only retrievable by people that have access to your browser.
